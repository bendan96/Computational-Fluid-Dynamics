# Key Findings

### Mesh Independence Study
- **Full Domain**: Four mesh densities tested (20×200 to 80×200). Centreline velocity improved from 1.488 m/s (Coarse, 0.8% error) to 1.500 m/s (Very Fine, 0% error). Fine mesh (60×200) selected as optimal.
- **Half Domain**: Three mesh densities tested (20×200 to 60×200). Excellent convergence even with coarse mesh (1.497 m/s, 0.2% error).

### Aerodynamic / Flow Results
- **Centreline Velocity**: Matched exact analytical value of **1.5 m/s**.
- **Velocity Profile**: Perfectly parabolic shape achieved in both domains.
- **Symmetry Advantage**: Half-domain model delivered near-identical accuracy with significantly lower computational cost.
- **Wall Shear Stress**: τ_w ≈ 0.424 Pa (full) and 0.41 Pa (half), consistent with laminar theory.

**Conclusion**: Mesh independence was successfully demonstrated. The study validates the reliability of the CFD setup.
